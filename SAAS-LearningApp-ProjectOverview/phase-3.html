Phase 3 of 5

Expo / React Native port

Move from web-only to native iOS and Android apps while reusing as much logic as possible.

Goals

  • Provide a high-quality native app experience for Lumi Plus families.
  • Reuse the reading engine, content, and sync logic between web and native.
  • Unlock offline-first usage on phones and tablets.
