Phase 4 – Lumi Plus (parent subscriptions)

Lumi Plus is an optional parent subscription that adds premium value (extra features and content) on top of free access and Lumi Schools access.

Goals

  • Enable parents to subscribe to Lumi Plus on monthly or annual plans.
  • Introduce paywalls around premium features while keeping a meaningful free tier.
  • Integrate billing (Stripe or RevenueCat) into the auth and account foundation from Phase 2.

Lumi Plus features

Lumi Plus (parent subscription) is defined in the roadmap as:

  • Full content library.
  • iOS + Android native app.
  • Offline mode (cached audio).
  • Progress dashboard.
  • Priority feature access (annual).
  • Founding family badge (annual).

Design questions

  • What is the exact boundary between free vs Lumi Plus features?
  • How visible is the upgrade path inside the learner experience vs parent settings?
  • Stripe vs RevenueCat for subscription management and renewals?
