Monetization

How LekkeLeer makes money: Lumi Plus parent subscriptions and Lumi Schools (annual school licences).

Parent subscription – Lumi Plus

Optional direct-to-parent subscription that adds premium value on top of free access and Lumi Schools access.

PlanPriceKey features
MonthlyR79/moFull content, iOS + Android app, offline mode, progress dashboard
AnnualR599/yrSame + priority feature access, founding family badge (≈ R49.90/mo, 37% savings)

Lumi Plus features (roadmap):

  • Full content library.
  • iOS + Android native app.
  • Offline mode (cached audio).
  • Progress dashboard.
  • Priority feature access (annual).
  • Founding family badge (annual).

Lumi Schools – annual, per school

Lumi Schools: annual licences for schools with teacher dashboards and per-learner progress. Learners can use their school-linked account at school or at home while the school licence is active (within the school’s seat allocation).

TierPriceLearnersKey features
SmallR8,500/yrUp to 100CAPS content, Afrikaans + English, teacher dashboard, per-learner progress, email support
MediumR16,000/yr101–400+ Multi-class, class stats/exports, priority support, onboarding
LargeR26,000/yr401++ Grade-level reporting, custom content, dedicated account manager, SLA

Revenue streams (summary)

  • Lumi Plus: recurring subscription revenue from parents (monthly and annual).
  • Lumi Schools: annual contracts per school, invoiced on licence tier.
