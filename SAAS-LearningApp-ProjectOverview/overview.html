The big picture

A learning platform that follows the child

LekkeLeer connects schoolwork and home practice in one place — what it is, who it serves, and where it's going.

What LekkeLeer is

A CAPS-based platform that ties classroom work, homework, and enrichment together.

🏫

In-class schoolwork

Teachers run weekly activities in class and see progress in near real time.

🏠

Homework

The same week's homework is available online at home, so learners don't miss work when a paper page is forgotten.

✨

Supplementary enrichment

Optional extra practice and enrichment for learners who want to go further within the CAPS structure.

🎒

Home schooling

Schools can enrol home-schooled learners into their LekkeLeer ecosystem so they receive the same weekly work and activities.

Who this overview is for

  • Parents & learners — understand what to do in class, at home, and for extra enrichment.
  • Funders & partners — understand the roadmap, impact, and monetization.
  • Schools — see how school licences and teacher dashboards will work.
  • Collaborators — quickly grasp the tech stack, content pipeline, and phases.
  • Future you — a static reference for decisions and the long-term plan.

Status

Content

Afrikaans reading

Term 1, Weeks 1–8 reading sentences (Lees sinne), with more terms and subjects on the way.

Features

Read, hear, track

Tap-to-hear, karaoke-style highlighting, read-aloud with speech recognition, and progress saving.

Platform

Web today, native next

A web app now, with a clear path to Expo / React Native for iOS and Android.
