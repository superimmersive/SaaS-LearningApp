Overview

High-level picture of LekkeLeer – what it is, who it serves, and where it is going.

What LekkeLeer is

LekkeLeer is a CAPS-based learning platform that connects schoolwork and home practice in one place.

  • In-class schoolwork (Lumi Schools): teachers run weekly activities in class and see progress in near real time.
  • Homework (Lumi Schools): the same week’s homework is available online at home, so learners don’t miss work when a paper page is forgotten.
  • Supplementary enrichment (Lumi Plus): optional extra practice and enrichment for learners who want to go further within the CAPS structure.
  • Home schooling (Lumi Schools): schools can enroll home-schooled learners into their Lumi ecosystem so they receive the same weekly work and activities.

Who this overview is for

  • Parents & learners: understand what to do in class, at home, and for extra enrichment.
  • Funders & partners: understand roadmap, impact, and monetization.
  • Schools: see how school licences and teacher dashboards will work.
  • Collaborators: quickly grasp tech stack, content pipeline, and phases.
  • Future you: a static reference for decisions and long-term plan.

Where we are now

  • Status: MVP live, early access, gathering feedback.
  • Content: Term 1, Weeks 1–8 Afrikaans reading sentences (Lees sinne).
  • Core features: tap-to-hear, karaoke-style highlighting, read-aloud with speech recognition, progress saving.
  • Platform: Web app, with a path to Expo / React Native for iOS and Android.

How the pieces fit together

  • Monetization: Lumi Schools (annual school licences with home access) plus an optional parent subscription (Lumi Plus). See Monetization.
