Lumi Schools

Annual Lumi Schools tiers with teacher dashboards, per-learner progress, reporting, and home access for learners.

Goals

  • Sell LekkeLeer as an annual licence per school instead of only to individual parents.
  • Home access included: learners can use their school-linked account at school or at home while the school licence is active.
  • Provide teachers with a dashboard for per-learner and class-wide progress.
  • Support multiple classes, grades, and reporting needs at different tiers.

Data model sketch

schools → classes → learners (linked to existing sentence_results)

  • schools: name, licence tier, expiry, capacity.
  • classes: class name, grade, school_id.
  • school_users: links users (teachers) to schools.
  • class_learners: links learners to classes.

How Lumi Schools is used

From sign-up to day-to-day classroom use and reporting.

1 · Buying & onboarding
  • A school chooses a tier (Small/Medium/Large) and signs an annual agreement.
  • You create the school record, teacher accounts, and classes (e.g. Grade 1A, 1B, 2A).
  • Learners are added or imported and attached to classes under that school.
2 · Day-to-day use
  • Teachers log in and pick the current term/week focus.
  • Learners use LekkeLeer during reading periods or lab/tablet sessions.
  • All activity is automatically linked to each learner and class for reporting.
3 · Monitoring & intervention
  • Per-learner views show who is behind or struggling with specific content.
  • Class-level summaries highlight overall progress and problem areas.
  • Group learners for extra support and export CSV/Excel for reports.
4 · School / district view
  • Grade-level reporting for HODs and principals.
  • Custom content requests and curriculum tweaks for partner schools.
  • SLA and dedicated account management for training and rollout.
🏠

Home access (Option 1)

  • Learners can log in with their school-linked account from home or school.
  • Access stays active while the school's Lumi Schools licence is active and the learner is within the seat allocation.
  • Lumi Plus becomes additive value (extra features/content), not a requirement for home access.
