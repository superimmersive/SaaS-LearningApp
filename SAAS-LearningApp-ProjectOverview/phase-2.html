Auth & accounts foundation

Lay down shared auth and account rails for parents and schools — multi-tenant identity, roles, and access.

Goals

  • Add authentication so each household and school can have its own accounts and roles.
  • Establish a shared account and organisation model (parents, learners, teachers, schools).
  • Prepare clean integration points for subscriptions and billing without turning on a full paywall yet.

Scope

From the roadmap.

ItemNotes
Supabase AuthEmail/password or magic link sign-in
Account modelHouseholds, learners, schools, and roles
Multi-tenant wiringLink users to schools/classes where needed
🔑

This phase is intentionally plumbing-first: it makes LekkeLeer plug-and-play for both parents and schools from the start, so Lumi Plus and Lumi Schools can switch on cleanly later.
